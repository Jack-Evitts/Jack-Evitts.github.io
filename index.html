About Me

I am a recent graduate from the University of Kent, having studied for a Research Masters in Astronomy specialising in Star Formation. I have previously achieved a 1st class Integrated Masters with Hons in Astrophysics from the University of Kent, with a year in the USA (University of North Carolina at Charlotte).

Through my education, I have developed a strong ability to manipulate data, from cleaning and analysis to presenting, using a variety of media. Proficient in Python and SQL, I have over 2 years experience in handling Time-series data, utilising tools such as Numpy and Pandas to extract meaningful results from large and complex data sets.

Send Mail Project

This project allows the user to send an email from their gmail account via python using the smtplib python library. The email is encrypted and secure, with usernames and passwords passed to the function through environment variables. Multiple files and/or images are able to be attached before sending and multiple recipients may be specified.
